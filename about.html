About

As a young woman in her yearly 20s, who has visited more than 15 countries around the world and met great people while doing so, I am well aware of how hard it is to find a partner that truly understands you. This lead me to the idea of creating a company around the perception of being able to find a true connection with a person through a holiday both of you would never forget!
Mingle is a travel agency that doesn’t just offer great destinations to visit, but also matches couples together based on a quiz that each person takes when booking. The all-inclusive trips provide the perfect atmosphere to connect with the other person, have an unforgettable experience, and who knows? Maybe even fall in love!

Vision

In a world where people sometimes feel all alone and afraid, we believe that you should take more chances, becasue only then you can actually live.
